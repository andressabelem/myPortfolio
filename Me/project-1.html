Virtual Museum of FACISA

The Faculdade de Ciências da Saúde (FACISA) is a UFRN institution located in the city of Santa Cruz/RN - Brazil, inaugurated in 2008.

Visit Site

Objective

The goal of this project was to create a fun and attractive look for young people and the general public.

Colors

Inspired by the theme of the project, "10 years of FACISA in Trairi: remaking paths, treading new routes" I added colors representing a day pass and a dotted trail connecting the photos, to give museum visitors a sense of walking through the history of the college.

Visual Elements

The elements of the landscape were inspired by the Northeastern backlands of Brazil, where the college is located. Some of these elements were made from an art that adorns one of the walls of FACISA, created by Professor and Artist Marcos Andruchak.
