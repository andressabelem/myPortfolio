Ocean

Ocean is a personal project for the study and practice of design, HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Objective

Design and develop a magical look of the sea and its creatures, specifically venomous animals, pointing out some interesting facts about them.

Colors

For the background I used two shades of blue with a radial gradient, the idea was to create an effect of sunlight entering the water, which fades and gets darker as we scroll down, like we were diving deeper into the sea.

For the title and subtitle, I chose other shades of blue, that contrast subtly with the background, as for the text, the white color can provide stronger contrast, making it easier to read.
